What resources are available for small business loans?
10:05 AM

There are several resources available for small business loans through the U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA) and other government programs:

1. SBA 7(a) Loan Program: The SBA's primary program for providing financial assistance to small businesses. These loans can be used for working capital, equipment, and real estate.

2. SBA Microloan Program: Provides loans up to $50,000 to help small businesses and certain non-profit childcare centers start up and expand.

3. Economic Injury Disaster Loans (EIDL): Low-interest loans to help businesses recover from declared disasters.

To get started, I recommend visiting the official SBA website at sba.gov/funding-programs/loans or contacting your local SBA office for personalized guidance.
